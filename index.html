— Two Sides of the Studio —

Style Directions

Every client sits somewhere on this line — sharp and boardroom-ready, rooted and celebratory, or a mix of both. Here's the range I design across.

Corporate

Clean tailoring built for the boardroom and beyond — cropped blazers over full skirts, sharp trouser suits, and structured silhouettes in neutral tones. Details like contrast ties, gold hardware, and precise seaming keep it polished without feeling stiff.

  • Tailored blazers
  • Wide-leg trousers
  • Structured skirts

Native

Bold Ankara and lace pieces built for celebration — fitted corseted bodices, dramatic bell sleeves, and layered ruffle skirts in high-contrast prints. Made to move on the dance floor and hold up in every photo.

  • Corseted waists
  • Lace panel sleeves
  • Tiered ruffle skirts
